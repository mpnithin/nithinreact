College of engineering Trivandrum

The College of Engineering Trivandrum, commonly known as CET, is the first engineering college in Kerala, situated in Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala. It was founded in 1939 during the reign of the Travancore King, Chithira Thirunal Balarama Varma.

golden jubilee celebration

Thiruvananthapuram: Students from the 1970 batch of College of Engineering, Trivandrum are celebrating their alumni’s golden jubilee year. The celebration, named Mahasangamam, began with a ‘Gurupooja’ where former professors of CET were honoured. The celebrations were inaugurated by former Isro chairman G Madhavan Nair at 3pm on Thursday.

“The contribution of our batch — in terms of academics, sports and cultural activities — were significant to the growth of CET as a pioneering educational institution in our country. So instead of merely celebrating the golden jubilee year of our batch, we thought of doing something unique and productive for our college. Only through such deeds we can be remembered by our college,” said S Gopakumar, an alumnus of the batch.
